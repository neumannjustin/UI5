Enum sap.m.FlexDirection

Available directions for flex layouts.

Field Summary
sap.m.FlexDirection.ColumnElements are layed out along the direction of the block axis (usually top to bottom).
sap.m.FlexDirection.ColumnReverseElements are layed out along the reverse direction of the block axis (usually bottom to top).
sap.m.FlexDirection.InheritInherits the value from its parent.
sap.m.FlexDirection.RowElements are layed out along the direction of the inline axis (text direction).
sap.m.FlexDirection.RowReverseElements are layed out along the reverse direction of the inline axis (against the text direction).

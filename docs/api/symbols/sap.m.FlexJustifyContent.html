Enum sap.m.FlexJustifyContent

Available options for the layout of elements along the main axis of the flexbox layout.

Field Summary
sap.m.FlexJustifyContent.CenterBox items are packed toward the center of the line.
sap.m.FlexJustifyContent.EndBox items are packed toward the end of the line.
sap.m.FlexJustifyContent.InheritInherits the value from its parent.
sap.m.FlexJustifyContent.SpaceAroundBox items are evenly distributed in the line, with half-size spaces on either end.
sap.m.FlexJustifyContent.SpaceBetweenBox items are evenly distributed in the line.
sap.m.FlexJustifyContent.StartBox items are packed toward the start of the line.
